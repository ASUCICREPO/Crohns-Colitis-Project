Who We Are

Crohns-Colitis is the leading foundation dedicated to supporting people with inflammatory bowel disease.

Our Mission

To help people with IBD understand their condition and find the support they need to live full and active lives.

What We Do

We provide resources, support, and education to people with IBD throughout the community.

Areas We Cover

  • Crohn's disease support
  • Ulcerative colitis resources
  • Treatment information
  • Nutrition guidance
  • Support groups
  • Research updates
